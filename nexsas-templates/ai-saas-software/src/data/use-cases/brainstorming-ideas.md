### The challenge

Creative and product teams struggle to generate and structure ideas quickly. Traditional brainstorming can be time-consuming and uneven in quality. Teams need a way to explore directions, refine concepts, and align on next steps without losing momentum or clarity.

### The solution

- AI-powered idea generation and expansion
- Structured outlines and mind maps
- Role and audience-aware prompts
- Collaboration and versioning
- Export to docs, slides, and task trackers
- Integration with design and project tools

### The challenge

Events and conferences require a lot of written content—agendas, speaker bios, session descriptions, and promotional copy—often under tight deadlines and with many stakeholders.

### The solution

- Agenda and session description generation
- Speaker bio drafting and standardization
- Email and social promotional copy
- Badge and signage copy
- Multi-track and multi-day consistency
- Localization for international events

### The challenge

Scaling product descriptions across large catalogs while keeping copy clear, consistent, and SEO-friendly is difficult. Manual writing doesn't scale, and generic copy can hurt conversion and search visibility.

### The solution

- Bulk product description generation
- SEO-optimized titles and meta descriptions
- Brand voice and tone consistency
- Variant and category-aware copy
- Localization and multi-language support
- Integration with product feeds and CMS

### The challenge

Academic writing demands clarity, structure, and adherence to citation standards. Researchers and students often struggle to organize arguments, maintain consistent voice, and meet formatting requirements across different disciplines and publication formats.

### The solution

- Structured outlines and argument mapping
- Citation and reference formatting support
- Paraphrasing and clarity improvements
- Discipline-specific tone and terminology
- Draft expansion and section development
- Integration with reference managers and editors

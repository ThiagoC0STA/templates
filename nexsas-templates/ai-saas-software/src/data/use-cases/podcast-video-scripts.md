### The challenge

Creating engaging podcast and video scripts at scale is time-consuming and often inconsistent in tone and structure. Teams need to maintain quality while meeting tight production schedules and adapting content for different formats and platforms.

### The solution

- AI-assisted script drafting and outlines
- Tone and audience adaptation
- Segment and hook suggestions
- B-roll and cue note generation
- Multi-format export (podcast, YouTube, short-form)
- Collaboration and version control for scripts

### Personalization and scale

AI makes it possible to tailor messaging, offers, and experiences to segments or individuals. Using AI to personalize customer experiences at scale enables:

- Messaging and offers tailored to segments or individuals.
- Less reliance on manual rules for every case.
- Consistency and relevance across touchpoints.

### What to prioritize

Using AI to personalize customer experiences at scale works when you focus on:

- Clean, consented data and clear use cases.
- Testing and measurement so you know what actually improves outcomes.
- Respect for privacy and transparency.

Get these right before scaling touchpoints.

### Start with one channel

Pick one high-impact channel and iterate before expanding. To start with one channel effectively:

- Choose a high-impact channel and define success metrics.
- Run experiments and measure lift before scaling.
- Prioritize quality of personalization over quantity of touchpoints.

### Conclusion

Personalization at scale with AI is achievable with the right data, metrics, and ethics. Start with one channel, prove impact, and expand from there.

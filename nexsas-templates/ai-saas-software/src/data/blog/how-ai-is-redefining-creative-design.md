### Design and AI

AI is changing how concepts are generated, refined, and delivered. Design and AI together mean:

- Faster generation and refinement of concepts.
- New tools for layout, palette, and copy.
- The designer's role in vision, critique, and user focus remains central.

### New possibilities

How AI is redefining creative design shows up in concrete ways:

- Faster exploration of layouts, palettes, and copy.
- Consistency at scale across touchpoints.
- More time for research and iteration.

Designers who adopt these tools can deliver more without burning out.

### The human edge

Empathy, brand judgment, and accessibility decisions still depend on human designers. The human edge includes:

- Empathy and brand judgment.
- Accessibility and inclusive design decisions.
- Final say on what ships and how it aligns with vision.

AI amplifies; it doesn't replace that expertise.

### Conclusion

AI is redefining creative design by handling more of the execution while leaving strategy and soul to humans. Embrace the tools and double down on what only you can do.

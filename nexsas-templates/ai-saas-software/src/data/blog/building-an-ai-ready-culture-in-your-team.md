### Culture before tools

Adopting AI successfully starts with mindset, not tools. Building an AI-ready culture in your team begins with:

- Curiosity and willingness to experiment.
- Clear governance and ownership.
- Alignment on where AI helps and where it does not.

Tools follow once the culture is ready.

### Steps that work

Building an AI-ready culture in your team takes deliberate steps:

- Start with low-risk use cases and share wins.
- Train people on both capabilities and limitations.
- Define ownership and review processes for AI-assisted work.

Culture change takes time; consistency and transparency accelerate it.

### Leadership matters

Leaders who model learning and set clear expectations help teams move from fear or hype to practical use. Leadership can support this by:

- Modeling learning and experimentation.
- Setting clear expectations and boundaries.
- Celebrating responsible use and lessons learned.

### Conclusion

An AI-ready culture is the foundation for sustainable adoption. Invest in people first, then tools, and your team will be ready for whatever comes next.

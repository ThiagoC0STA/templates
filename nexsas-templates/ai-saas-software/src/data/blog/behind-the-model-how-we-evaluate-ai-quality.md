### Evaluating AI outputs

Evaluating AI outputs means looking at:

- Relevance and consistency for the use case.
- Safety and compliance with guidelines.
- Fit for your product and users.

We combine automated checks with human review.

### What we measure

Behind the model, how we evaluate AI quality comes down to:

- Correctness and completeness for the task.
- Bias, safety, and compliance with guidelines.
- Latency and cost at target scale.

Each dimension matters for production readiness.

### Continuous improvement

We run evals on every major change and sample production outputs. Continuous improvement involves:

- Running evals on every major change.
- Sampling production outputs for quality.
- Using the feedback loop to keep quality high as models and products evolve.

### Conclusion

Understanding how we evaluate AI quality is essential for shipping with confidence. Measure what matters, automate where you can, and keep humans in the loop for the rest.

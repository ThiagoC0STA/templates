### Ship faster with AI

Building an AI-powered MVP no longer requires training models from scratch. To ship AI products faster, you can rely on:

- Hosted APIs and existing models.
- Prompt engineering and lightweight customization.
- Early user feedback to validate the idea.

Use these to validate the idea and learn from users early.

### Practical approach

To go from idea to MVP and ship AI products faster, focus on:

- Defining the smallest valuable experience and one clear metric.
- Using hosted models and prompt engineering first; customize only if needed.
- Planning for iteration: prompts, data, and UX will evolve.

Speed to market matters more than perfection in the early stage.

### Learn then scale

Use the MVP to test demand and gather feedback. Then invest in areas such as:

- Robustness and error handling.
- Performance and latency at scale.
- Differentiation through data or UX.

### Conclusion

From idea to MVP, shipping AI products faster is achievable with the right approach. Start small, learn fast, and scale when you have proof.

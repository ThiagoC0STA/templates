### The state of AI in 2025

AI is now embedded in everyday tools, from search and email to design and code. The state of AI in 2025 is characterized by:

- Widespread use in search, email, design, and code.
- Stronger capabilities and higher expectations.
- More scrutiny on safety, bias, and transparency.

Understanding where it helps and where it falls short is essential.

### What to watch

What you should know about AI in 2025 includes these trends:

- Multimodal models that combine text, image, and video.
- Stricter regulation and transparency requirements.
- More focus on cost and performance at scale.

Stay curious and keep testing new tools with real workflows.

### How to stay ahead

Staying ahead in 2025 means paying attention to:

- New model releases and capability shifts.
- Regulatory and compliance updates.
- Real-world use cases in your industry.

### Conclusion

AI in 2025 is both more capable and more scrutinized. Stay informed, experiment responsibly, and align your use of AI with your values and your customers' expectations.
